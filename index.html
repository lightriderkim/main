Technical Domains

Core R&H Topics

Covering the full spectrum of ride comfort, handling stability, and chassis dynamics — from fundamental theory to test methodology and simulation.

Ride Comfort

Vibration isolation, body bounce, pitch, and roll dynamics. ISO 2631-compliant comfort metrics and road input characterization.

Handling Stability

Steady-state and transient handling, yaw dynamics, understeer/oversteer characterization, and stability limit analysis.

Suspension Design

Kinematic and compliance (K&C) analysis, spring/damper tuning, linkage geometry, and anti-dive/squat behaviors.

Tire Dynamics

Pacejka Magic Formula, tire contact patch, cornering stiffness, combined slip, and tire model parameterization.

NVH Analysis

Noise, vibration, harshness assessment. Structure-borne and airborne noise paths, modal analysis, and frequency response functions.

Test & Simulation

Proving ground test procedures, objective metrics (ISO/FMVSS), MBS simulation, and virtual testing methodologies.
